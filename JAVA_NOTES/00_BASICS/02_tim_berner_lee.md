# Tim Berners-Lee (the man who change ruled the internet)

## About Tim:

Full Name: Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee
Born: June 1955, London, England
Education: Physics degree from oxford university

Tim was working as a software engineer at CERN(the Europen Organition for Nuclear Research)

---

## Problem he faced:

1. Cern had thousands or researchers across the world.
2. They used diff computers, different software and formats.
3. Sharing documents and research was a nightmare.
4. There was no standard way to link information.

---

## What things he did:

1. Invented the www in 1989 at cern.
2. He created HTML, HTTP and URL.
3. First Website: https://info.cern.ch in 6 aug 1991.
4. In 1993, CERN annouced that the www technology would be free for everyone.
5. He founded the w3c at MIT in 1994, It is the international standard body for web.
