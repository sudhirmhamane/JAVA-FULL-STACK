# IS vs IS NOT

These operator are used to compare the null values.

- Syntax:

WHERE col/exp IS/IS NOT NULL;

---

- **NOTE**

1. NULL is not a value.
2. NULL means not zere.
3. NULL = NULL (False condition)

---

### Q1. WAQTD ALL THE DETAILS OF EMP, THE EMP WHOS COMM ARE NULL

ANSWER:

SELECT *
FROM emp
WHERE COMM IS NULL;

---

### Q2. WAQTD ALL THE DETAILS OF EMP, THE EMP WHO ARE NOT HAVING ANY REPORTING MANAGER.

ANSWER:

SELECT *
FROM emp
WHERE MGR IS NOT NULL;

---

### Q3. WAQTD ALL THE DETAILS OF EMP WHO ARE WORKING IN THE DEPT NO 10, 20 EXCEPT SALESMAN, MANAGER AND WHO ARE NOT HAVING ANY REPORTING MANAGER.

ANSWER:

SELECT *
FROM EMP
WHERE DEPTNO IN (10, 20) AND JOB NOT IN ('SALESMAN', 'MANAGER') AND MGR IS NOT NULL;
